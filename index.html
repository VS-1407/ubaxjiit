Welcome to UBA JIIT!

The Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, has launched the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA), a national program aimed at involving higher educational institutions in the sustainable development of villages.
Our UBA is a vibrant community of students passionate about learning, sharing knowledge, and organizing fun and educational activities.
